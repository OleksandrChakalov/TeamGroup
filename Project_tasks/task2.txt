Project Charter for Trip Planner Application:

Project Title: Go & See Trip Planner

Project Objectives:

To develop a web application that simplifies the process of trip planning and packing list creation.
To provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface for creating, editing and sharing travel itineraries.
To allow users to easily take and organize photos during their trip and add them to their itinerary.
To provide users with a tool to keep track of their travel preparations and to ensure that they do not forget anything important.
To provide a seamless user experience that saves time and reduces the stress associated with travel planning.


Project Goals:

Develop a user-friendly and intuitive interface for trip planning and packing list creation.
Implement a feature for taking and organizing photos during the trip.
Ensure that the application is secure and user data is protected.
Ensure that the application is scalable and can handle a large number of users.
Launch the application on schedule and within budget.


Target Audience:

Frequent travelers who want an easy-to-use tool to plan their trips.
Individuals who want to ensure they don't forget anything important when packing for their trip.
Travel enthusiasts who want to document their trips and share their experiences with others.


Stakeholders:

Project Manager: Responsible for overseeing the development process and ensuring that the project is completed on schedule and within budget.
Development Team: Responsible for designing, developing, and testing the application.
End Users: The individuals who will be using the application.
Investors: Individuals or organizations who have invested in the project.


Project Constraints:

Budget: The project budget is limited and all development activities must be completed within budget.
Time: The project must be completed within the given timeline.
Resources: The development team has limited resources and must work efficiently to complete the project.
Technical Constraints: The application must be compatible with a range of web browsers and devices, and must meet security standards.
